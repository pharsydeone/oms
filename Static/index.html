Orthopedic injuries can be especially complex. Patients with these types of injuries often have limited mobility due to pain, weakness and limited range of motion. At Orthopedic Medicine Specialists, we have the expertiese and training to help you regain your strength and mobility so you can heal quickly and completely.

Our goal is to partner with you and your primary physician to promote good health and minimize pain in your joints and bones. We take a compassionate, conservative approach to orthopedic care - pursuing all available surgical and non-surgical treatments that will reduce your recovery time and help you return to your active, pain-free lifestyle. From diagnosis and treatment to surgery and rehabilitation for conditions such as carpal tunnel, trigger finger, ganglion cysts, and more, our staff offers you the choices and individualized orthopedic care you need to heal quickly and comfortably.

Orthopedic Medicine Specialists is part of Texas Health Physicans Group, a physican network of more than 800 healthcare providers in North Texas.
